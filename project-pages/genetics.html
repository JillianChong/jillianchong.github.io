DNA Transcription Simulator
Overview
DNA Transcription Simulator is a first-person game, where the player follows the process of DNA Transcription. The goal of the game is to learn about the three steps of DNA transcription: initiation, elongation, and termination. In each stage, the player moves around to solve puzzles using their knowledge of the corresopnding stage of DNA transcription. Over the span of a week, I collaborated with three classmates to build a fully-functioning game. My responsibilities included building the initiation stage; implementing player functionality, like picking up and placing objects; and piecing the different stages together. This game was built for the final project of my high school genetics class. As a developer, the goal was to find a way to educate users in an engaging manner while applying my knowledge from my high school game development class.
Built with: Unity and C#
